About this framework

This is the interactive layer (v6.0) of a multi-pass framework for understanding modern pharmaceutical innovation. The framework was built in six passes:

  • v1.0 · Company Layer — Top 15 pharma + watchlist
  • v2.0 · Modality Layer — 12 platforms from small molecules to PROTACs
  • v3.0 · Therapeutic Area Layer — 7 TAs with commercialization dynamics
  • v4.0 · China Deep Dive — innovation ecosystem + deal flow + BIOSECURE
  • v5.0 · Structured Data Layer — normalized Excel database
  • v6.0 · This artifact

The data layer (v5) is the editable master. Update the Excel workbook, regenerate the embedded JSON, and the artifact reflects the changes.

The intended use is industry/strategy understanding for venture-building in Singapore — particularly the China-to-SEA arbitrage thesis that emerged across the framework.
